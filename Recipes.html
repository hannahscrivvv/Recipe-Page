Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chocolate chip cookies are delicious because they are chewy and refreshing with milk. Another reason they are good is because when cooking them it's not that messy and bake time is fast. Find more information here.

Ingredients Directions
1/2 cup (113g) unsalted butter, melted Place melted butter in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment (or a large bowl if using a hand mixer). Add granulated and brown sugars and mix on low speed until the mixture is smooth. Mix in egg and vanilla extract and mix on medium speed until combined.
1/3 cup (66 g) granulated sugar Mix in baking soda and salt, then slowly mix in flour and mix just until the batter is smooth and comes together. Be sure to scrape the sides of the bowl during mixing. Slowly mix in chocolate chips.
1/2 cup (104g) packed light brown sugar Line a cookie sheet with a silicone baking mat or parchment paper. Scoop 2 tablespoon balls of dough onto the cookie sheet. Spacing doesn’t matter because you will be chilling the dough. Cover with plastic wrap and chill for at least 2 hours.
1 large egg Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a second cookie sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.
1 teaspoon (5ml) vanilla extract Remove the chilled cookie dough balls from the refrigerator and space them 2-inches apart on the cookie sheets. Bake (2 tablespoon sized cookies) for 11-15 minutes, or until the edges are a light golden and the tops are no longer glossy. Let cool on the cookie sheets at least 10 minutes before removing.
1/2 teaspoon baking soda Turn oven off.
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups (186g) all-purpose flour
1 1/2 cups (255g) chocolate chips (semi-sweet or milk)

Sugar Cookies

Sugar cookies are a great cookie to eat anytime and they are delicious. Sugar cookies cook very fast and many people love to eat them. Find more information here.

Ingredients Directions
3/4 cup unsalted butter (1 1/2 sticks), softened Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F and line baking sheets with parchment paper.
1 cup granulated sugar Place the butter and sugar in a large mixing bowl and beat on medium-high speed until very pale and fluffy (about 3 to 5 minutes).
1 large egg Add the egg, and beat on medium-high speed until incorporated.
1 large egg yolk Stir in the egg yolk and vanilla extract until combined.
2 teaspoons vanilla extract Scrape the bottom and sides of the bowl with a silicone spatula, then add the flour, cornstarch, baking soda, and salt.
2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour Stir together until incorporated.
1/4 cup cornstarch Use a 1.5-tablespoon cookie scoop to drop balls of cookie dough on the prepared baking sheets, allowing two inches in between for spreading.
1 teaspoon baking soda Flatten the balls slightly with the palm of your hand.
1/2 teaspoon kosher salt Bake the cookies (one sheet at a time) in the center rack of the oven for 8 to 12 minutes, or until the edges are set and just barely beginning to turn golden. (Take care not to over-bake, or the cookies will not be as soft.)
Cool on the baking sheet and transfer to an airtight container for storage.

Double Chocolate Chip

Double chocolate chip cookies are a great one for you if you love chocolate. These cookies are best when dipped in milk after a nice meal. Find more information here.

Ingredients Directions
1/4 Cup Coconut Flour Prepare a cookie sheet with parchment paper or a silicone baking mat.
3/4 tsp Baking Powder In a small bowl, combine coconut flour, baking powder, and salt, (sifting the coconut flour if yours is lumpy). Set aside.
1/8 tsp Salt In a heatproof bowl, melt together the chocolate and butter over a double boiler (or use a heatproof, microwave-safe bowl and melt very carefully in 20-second increments). Set aside to cool.
3 oz Unsweetened Chocolate In the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with a whisk attachment, combine the egg, allulose, vanilla, and instant coffee. It will seem like too little liquid at first! Whip the mixture on high speed for 5-7 minutes. It will become very light and thick, and the allulose and instant coffee will have begun to dissolve into the egg.
2 Tbsp Butter Using a rubber spatula, fold the chocolate mixture into the egg mixture. Then fold in the coconut flour mixture, followed by the chocolate chips.
1 Large Egg, room temperature Scoop the cookies onto the prepared baking sheet and bake at 325 degrees F for 12-15 minutes.
3/4 Cup Allulose
1 tsp Vanilla
1 tsp Instant Coffee Powder (unsweetened)
1/2 Cup KNOW Foods Sugar Free Chocolate Chips

Peanut Butter Cookies

Peanut butter cookies are wonderful. They are wonderful because of the taste and the preparation is very easy. Another good thing about them is the bake time is very quick. Find more information here.

Ingredients Directions
1 1/3 cup (188g) all-purpose flour Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Line two 18 by 13-inch baking sheets with silicone baking liners or parchment paper.
3/4 tsp baking soda In a medium mixing bowl whisk together flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.
1/2 tsp baking powder In the bowl of an electric stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment cream together butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until combined.
1/4 tsp salt Mix in peanut butter then blend in egg and vanilla. With mixer set on low speed slowly add in flour mixture and mix just until combined.
1/2 cup (113g) unsalted butter, softened Scoop dough out and shape into balls (30 grams each or nearly 2 Tbsp) then place on baking sheets spacing them 2-inches apart.
1/2 cup (105g) granulated sugar Using a long pronged fork flatten cookies slightly then turn fork going opposite direction and flatten just slightly again (it should create that criss cross pattern).
1/2 cup (110g) packed light brown sugar Bake cookies in preheated oven, one sheet at a time, for about 9 minutes (cookies will appear pale and slightly under-baked, they'll continue to cook slightly as they cool).
3/4 cup (185g) creamy peanut butter Let cool on baking sheet 5 minutes then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely. Store cookies and an airtight container.
1 large egg
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Peppermint White Chocolate Chip Cookies

Peppermint white chocolate cookies are a wonderful amazing snack for holidays and seasonal party's. Even though the taste might be a little strong from the peppermints the cookies are still delicious. Find more information here.

Ingredients Directions
1/2 cup butter (only real butter, not low fat), room temperature Mix the butter and sugars in a bowl.
1/2 cup brown sugar, packed Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
1/2 cup white sugar Mix the flour, baking soda and salt in another bowl.
1 egg Mix the dry ingredients into the wet until they are just incorporated.
1 teaspoon vanilla extract Mix in the chocolate and the peppermint candy cane.
1 1/2 cup all purpose flour, sifted Chill the dough in the fridge for 30-60 minutes before placing it onto a parchment paper lined cookie sheet one table spoon at a time rolled up into balls.
1 teaspoon baking soda Bake in a 350F/180C preheated oven until just lightly golden brown around the edges, about 8-10 minutes.
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup white chocolate chips
1/2 cup peppermint candy cane, crushed
